Create Refresh Table 11


Steps(Refresh Date Table)
 On the Power BI Desktop ribbon, Click on Transform data
 Click on the New Source button, select Blank query from the list, and click on Connect
 Under properties in the Name box type in 'Refresh Date'
 On the Dax formula bar type ' =Date Time.LocalNow()'
 Click on the Convert to Table button
 After the refresh table pops up, rename the column by right-clicking and choosing to rename from the selection
 Click  on the Close & Apply button and save
