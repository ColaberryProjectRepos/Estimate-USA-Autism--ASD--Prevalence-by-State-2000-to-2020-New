Step (Calendar Table)
 On the Power BI desktop under Modeling click on the New table button
 Type the Dax formula below in the Dax formula bar, Click on the check mark, and save
 Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS (GENERATESERIES (2000, 2020, 1), "Date", DATE([Value], 1, 1))
